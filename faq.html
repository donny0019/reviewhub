Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. Here are some of the most common queries we receive.

Getting started is simple! Just click on the "Get Started" or "Sign Up" button on our website, fill out the registration form, and complete a brief qualification test. Once approved, you'll gain access to our dashboard of available app review opportunities.

We process payments through secure platforms like PayPal and direct bank transfer. Payments are made on a net-15 basis, meaning you will receive your earnings for all approved reviews from a given month by the 15th of the following month. You can track your earnings and request a payout directly from your user dashboard.

No, joining ReviewHub is completely free. We do not charge any registration fees or take a cut from your earnings. Our revenue comes from the app developers who pay for our platform to access quality user feedback.

Most of our testing opportunities require a modern smartphone or tablet running a recent version of iOS or Android. Some specialized tests may require specific devices, which will be clearly stated in the project description. During sign-up, you'll be asked to list the devices you own to be matched with relevant opportunities.

The number of opportunities varies based on your demographic profile, the devices you own, and the current needs of our clients. We recommend keeping your profile updated and checking the dashboard regularly for new projects. Active members with high-quality reviews are often prioritized for new and higher-paying tests.

Our mission is to provide a reliable and flexible way for individuals to earn income by sharing their valuable opinions on the latest mobile applications.
