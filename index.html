5 Proven Ways to Make Money Online Reviewing Apps

By Jane Smith • Last Updated: July 19, 2025

In today's digital age, your smartphone is more than just a communication deviceâ€”it's a potential source of income. The app economy is booming, and developers are willing to pay for valuable feedback to improve their products. This guide explores five legitimate and proven methods for making money by reviewing and testing mobile applications.

1. Join User Testing Platforms

User testing platforms are the most straightforward way to get started. These companies act as middlemen, connecting app developers with everyday users like you. Your job is to perform specific tasks within an app and provide authentic feedback on the experience.

How it Works:

  • You sign up on platforms like UserTesting, TryMyUI, or Userlytics.
  • You complete a profile and often a sample test to qualify.
  • You receive notifications for new testing opportunities that match your demographic.
  • Tests typically involve recording your screen and voice as you navigate an app and speak your thoughts aloud.

Earning Potential: Payouts typically range from $10 to $20 for a 15-20 minute test. It's a flexible way to earn, though the availability of tests can vary.

2. Offer Your Services on Freelance Platforms

Platforms like Upwork, Fiverr, and Freelancer are hubs for developers seeking specific testing services. You can create a profile that highlights your skills as an app reviewer and bid on relevant projects.

How to Succeed:

  • Create a professional profile detailing your experience, the devices you own (iOS, Android, etc.), and any specialized knowledge.
  • Start with smaller projects to build a strong portfolio and positive reviews.
  • Offer specialized services, such as localization testing (checking language and cultural appropriateness) or accessibility testing.

Earning Potential: You set your own rates. New freelancers might start around $15-$25 per hour, while experienced testers can command much higher fees.

3. Create Your Own Review Content

For the entrepreneurial-minded, creating your own platform is the most scalable long-term strategy. This involves building an audience around your app reviews through a blog, YouTube channel, or social media presence.

Monetization Methods:

  • Affiliate Marketing: Earn a commission when someone downloads or purchases an app through your unique link.
  • Advertising: Place ads on your blog or YouTube channel (e.g., Google AdSense).
  • Sponsorships: As your audience grows, developers may pay you to feature their app in a sponsored review.

Earning Potential: This path has the highest potential but requires significant upfront effort in content creation and audience building. Successful app reviewers can earn a full-time income.

4. Work Directly with App Developers

Cutting out the middleman can lead to higher-paying, more consistent work. Many startups and established companies hire freelance testers directly to get in-depth feedback on their apps before a major launch or update.

How to Find Gigs:

  • Network on professional sites like LinkedIn.
  • Explore company career pages for "QA Tester" or "Beta Tester" roles.

Earning Potential: This can range from hourly rates ($20-$50/hr) to per-project payments.
